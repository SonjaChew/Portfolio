Prime Video

Streaming Video Service Redesign
Role: UX/UI Designer
Team: Sonja Chew, Deepa Bharadwaj, Lydia Huang, Hattie Chau, & Adriana Garzon
Duration: 2.5 weeks




Overview

Prime video is an American Internet video on demand service that is developed, owned and operated by Amazon.
We saw an opportunity to hone in on the social component from a marketing standpoint and redesigned the website for a better personalized, fluid experience.



The Problem


Due to the COVID-19 shelter-in-place, the use of streaming platforms has skyrocketed. Users are having difficulty finding content they enjoy as well as having a way to share this content with their friends and family. They feel that Prime Video (at the time) was not reflective of their personal interests and their desire to connect.
